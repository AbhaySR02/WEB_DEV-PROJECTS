mashed potato

desciption:

This mashed potato recipe is perfect if you're looking for good, old-fashioned mashed potatoes. Made with just potatoes, milk, butter, and optional garlic, this go-to recipe makes a classic holiday dish. Learn how to make mashed potatoes that come out smooth, creamy, and delicious every time! Serve with gravy or extra butter on top.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and chopped into 1-inch cubes
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 clove garlic, minced (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Gravy or extra butter for serving (optional)

steps:
  1. Place the chopped potatoes in a large pot and add enough cold water to cover them. Bring to
  2. a boil over high heat, then reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer for 15-
  3. 20 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender when pierced with a fork.
  4. Drain the potatoes and return them to the pot. Add the milk, butter, garlic (if
  5. using), salt, and pepper. Mash the potatoes with a potato masher or a fork until
  6. they're smooth and creamy. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed.
  7. Serve the mashed potatoes hot, with gravy or extra butter on the side if desired.
